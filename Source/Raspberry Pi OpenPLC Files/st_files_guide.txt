.st files are the files running on the 4 Raspberry Pi on the table through the OpenPlC application.

Raspberry Pi 192.168.1.81
    *uses 107890.st
        -substation program
    *controls substaion for first district (Houses -> Theatre; Bank -> Fire; Police)
    *contains required transmission and distribution coils, along with required coil for traffic light

Raspberry Pi 192.168.1.82
    *uses 406635.st
        *PROGRAM PLC_2_3_4
    *controls hospital generator

Raspberry Pi 192.168.1.83
    *uses 692121.st
        -PROGRAM PLC_2_3_4
    *controls police/fire generator

Raspberry Pi 192.168.1.98
    *uses 706957.st
        -PROGRAM traffic_light
    *controls traffic light functionality
    *requires coils from 192.168.1.81
